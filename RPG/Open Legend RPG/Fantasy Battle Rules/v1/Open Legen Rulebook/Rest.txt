
   $$$ Recovery Dice $$$$$:

      - (HR) after each full rest, a character regains all of their Stamina, plus a portion
        of their MP/HP.

        - Full Rest:
          MP RECOVERED = 25%(rounded down)MaxMP + 1,5*MP Dice (rounded down)
          HP RECOVERED = 20%(rounded down)MaxHP +     HP Dice
          Stamina RECOVERED = 100-(20 per level of fatigue)% of Maximum Stamina

        - Short Rest:
          HP RECOVERED = 0
          MP RECOVERED = 0,25*MP Dice (rounded down)
          Stamina RECOVERED = 1,5* HP Dice
