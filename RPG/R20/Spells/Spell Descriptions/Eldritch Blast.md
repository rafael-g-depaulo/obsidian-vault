### Energy Blast
*Truque*
___
CAST_TIME: padrão
RANGE: 20sqr
TARGET: 1 criatura
DURATION: instantânea.

A beam of crackling energy streaks toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 radiant damage.
