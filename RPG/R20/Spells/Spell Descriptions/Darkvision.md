### Darkvision
*2º Círculo*
___
CAST_TIME: padrão
RANGE: Toque
TARGET: 1 criatura
DURATION: cena.

You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark.  
For the duration, that creature has darkvision out to a range of 12sqr (18m).
